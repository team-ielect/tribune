How It Works

Our simple four-step process makes election management easy and efficient.

1

Create Election

Set up your election with details like title, date, time, location, and number of booths.

2

Add Candidates & Voters

Register candidates and voters with their information and generate unique QR codes for verification.

3

Conduct Polling

Activate the election during the specified time and allow voters to cast their votes securely.

4

Publish Results

Review the results and publish them for all voters to see with detailed statistics.
